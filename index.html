History

ISPSC is a comprehensive six-campus institution of higher learning mandated to give professional and technical training both in the undergraduate and graduate levels in the fields of economics, agriculture, fisheries, and industrial fields. It is also mandated to provide research, extension, and production services for the development of the Province of Ilocos Sur and the nation. The College has been existing for decades. The main campus is situated in Santa Maria, Ilocos Sur and the five other campuses are strategically located in Candon City, Narvacan, Santiago, Tagudin, and Sta. Cruz.
